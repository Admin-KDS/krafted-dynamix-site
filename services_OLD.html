Services

We specialise in Microsoft Dynamics 365, Power Platform, and Azure — from greenfield implementations to long-term support.

Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations (D365 F&O)

We help you implement, stabilise, and enhance Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations, with a balanced focus on both technical design and finance process alignment.

  • New implementations and country roll-outs
  • Fit-gap analysis and solution design
  • Localisation and regulatory requirements
  • Customisations using extensions and best practices
  • Data migration, cutover planning, and post-go-live support

Dynamics 365 CRM / Customer Engagement

Build a CRM landscape that supports your business development and client management lifecycle from first contact to long-term relationship.

  • Sales pipeline setup (leads, opportunities, and forecasting)
  • Case and service management
  • Custom entities, forms, and business rules
  • Integration with Microsoft 365 (Outlook, SharePoint, Teams)
  • Dashboards, KPIs, and reporting for leadership

Power Platform – Power Apps & Power Automate

We design low-code apps and workflows that complement your Dynamics 365 landscape and reduce dependence on spreadsheets and manual processes.

  • Model-driven apps built on Dataverse
  • Canvas apps for field and operational teams
  • Approval workflows and notifications with Power Automate
  • Integration with D365, SharePoint, and external systems
  • Environment strategy, security roles, and governance

Azure Integration & Cloud Services

Design and implement maintainable integrations using Azure, with proper monitoring and security baked in from day one.

  • Logic Apps and Azure Functions for integrations
  • Blob Storage for documents and image storage
  • Application Insights for logging and monitoring
  • Key Vault for secrets management
  • Error handling, retries, and alerting patterns

Support, Health Checks & Optimisation

Keep your Dynamics ecosystem healthy with structured support and periodic reviews of performance, customisations, and processes.

  • Health checks for performance and stability
  • Review of customisations and extensions
  • Cleanup of obsolete objects and unused features
  • Best practice and roadmap recommendations
  • Ongoing managed support with flexible hours
